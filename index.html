Brain Storm Ideas:


For ESO players

Unlike the Unofficial ESO Page https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online,

Our product will be much simpler and only loaded with ESO End Game in mind.


For Halo players

Who wants to visually see all the available armor options for customization in Halo Infinite.

Is a website that will have a database of all different Spartan customization options with images and videos.

Unlike HaloFashion Reddit https://www.reddit.com/r/HaloFashion/,
